This article is protected by copyright. All rights reserved.Atopic dermatitis (AD) is a chronic inflammatory skin disease. Patients with AD have increased infection risk, including skin infections and systemic infections. Dupilumab, a fully human monoclonal antibody, blocks the shared receptor component for interleukin-4 (IL-4) and IL-13. Dupilumab is approved for inadequately controlled moderate-to-severe AD.1.
