Currently, the emergence of a novel human coronavirus, SARS-CoV-2, has become a global health concern causing severe respiratory tract infections in humans. Crisis has been made worse by shortages of essential medical supplies, which has led to chemists and other researchers in all the world to works hard because there is no vaccine and no effective treatment. Coronavirus attack all the countries without exception but it make specificity for some part of the world such Italy, Spain, and before that, China and Iran. In this work we highlight to common parameters that influence with reactivity of virus which we depend to report the kinetic studies and our opinions towards the specificity of virus with Europeans people.
