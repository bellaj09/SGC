one case of coronavirus disease 2019 (covid-19) in a patient co-infected by hiv with a low cd4+ t-cell count. ¬© 2020 The AuthorsThe ongoing outbreak of COVID-19 that began in Wuhan, China, become an emergency of international concern when thousands of people were infected around the world. This study reports a case simultaneously infected by SARS-Cov-2 and HIV, which showed a longer disease course and slower generation of specific antibodies. This case highlights that a co-infection of SARS-Cov-2 and HIV may severely impair the immune system.
