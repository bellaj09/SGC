The current report summarizes case studies from an Office of Naval Research ONR funded project to compare the effects of Virtual Reality Graded Exposure Therapy VRGET with a treatment as usual control condition in active-duty Navy Corpsmen Navy SeaBees and Navy and Marine Corps Support Personnel. Details of the collaborative program between the Virtual Reality Medical Center VRMC and Naval Medical Center San Diego NMCSD will be discussed. Combat related post traumatic stress disorder: a multiple case report using virtual reality graded exposure therapy with physiological monitoring.
