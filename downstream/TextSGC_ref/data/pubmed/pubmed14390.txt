Generation Z is starting to reach college age. They have adopted technology from an early age and have a deep dependence on it; therefore they have become more drawn to the virtual world. M-learning has experienced huge growth in recent years both in the medical context and in medical and health sciences education. Ultrasound imaging is an important diagnosis technique in physiotherapy especially in sports pathology. M-learning systems could be useful tools for improving the comprehension of ultrasound concepts and the acquisition of professional competencies. The Ecofisio Mobile App for Assessment and Diagnosis Using Ultrasound Imaging for Undergraduate Health Science Students: Multicenter Randomized Controlled Trial.
