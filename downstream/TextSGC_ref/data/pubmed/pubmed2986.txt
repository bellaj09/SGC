This is a brief report of 4 paediatric cases of COVID-19 infection in Malaysia BACKGROUND: COVID-19 a coronavirus first detected in Wuhan China has now spread rapidly to over 60 countries and territories around the world infecting more than 85000 individuals. As the case count amongst children is low there is need to report COVID-19 in children to better understand the virus and the disease. COVID-19: Four Paediatric Cases in Malaysia.
