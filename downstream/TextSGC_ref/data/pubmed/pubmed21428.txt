Minimally invasive spine surgery MISS and endoscopic spine surgery have continually evolving indications in the cervical thoracic and lumbar spine. Endoscopic spine surgery entails treatment of disc disease stenosis spondylolisthesis radiculopathy and deformity. MISS involves complex motor skills in regions of variable anatomy. Simulator use has been proposed to aid in training and skill retention preoperative planning and intraoperative use. Virtual reality in spinal endoscopy: a paradigm shift in education to support spine surgeons.
