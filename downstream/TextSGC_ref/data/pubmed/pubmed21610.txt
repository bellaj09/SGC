Psychological Stress occurs when an individual perceives that environmental demands tax or exceed his or her adaptive capacity. Following this view that underlines the role of the situated experience of the patient in experiencing a stressor the European Union funded Interstress project http://www.interstress.eu is exploring the possible use of the virtual simulation technology offered by SecondLife http://www.secondlife.com to administer a stress management experiential training. The structure of the environment and the contents of the course are described and discussed. Learning Island: the development of a virtual reality system for the experiential training of stress management.
