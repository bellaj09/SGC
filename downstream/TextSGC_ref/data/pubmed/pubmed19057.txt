Dementia is a multifaceted disorder that impairs cognitive functions such as memory language and executive functions necessary to plan organize and prioritize tasks required for goal-directed behaviors. In most cases individuals with dementia experience difficulties interacting with physical and social environments. The purpose of this study was to establish ecological validity and initial construct validity of a fire evacuation Virtual Reality Day-Out Task VR-DOT environment based on performance profiles as a screening tool for early dementia. Ecological validity of virtual reality daily living activities screening for early dementia: longitudinal study.
