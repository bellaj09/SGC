Intraoral scanners IOS are devices for capturing direct optical impressions in dentistry. The purpose of this narrative review on the use of IOS was to: 1 identify the advantages/disadvantages of using optical impressions compared to conventional impressions; 2 investigate if optical impressions are as accurate as conventional impressions; 3 evaluate the differences between the IOS currently available commercially; 4 determine the current clinical applications/limitations in the use of IOS. Intraoral scanners in dentistry: a review of the current literature.
