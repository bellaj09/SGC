Impaired walking ability balance and fatigue are common problems for people with multiple sclerosis MS. The purpose of this case report is to describe the use of plan of care that included locomotor training using both a body weight support BWS with a treadmill TM and overground walking as well as a virtual reality VR-based balance intervention to improve walking ability balance and endurance for an individual with MS. Locomotor training and virtual reality-based balance training for an individual with multiple sclerosis: a case report.
