Prominent executive dysfunction can differentiate vascular dementia from Alzheimer disease AD. However it is unclear whether the Frontal Assessment Battery FAB screening tool can differentiate subcortical ischemic vascular disease SIVD from AD at the pre-dementia stage. In addition the neural correlates of FAB performance have yet to be clarified. Frontal Assessment Battery as a Useful Tool to Differentiate Mild Cognitive Impairment due to Subcortical Ischemic Vascular Disease from Alzheimer Disease.
