The coronavirus disease COVID-19 has been identified as the cause of an outbreak of respiratory illness in Wuhan Hubei Province China beginning in December 2019. As of 31 January 2020 this epidemic had spread to 19 countries with 11\u2009791 confirmed cases including 213 deaths. The World Health Organization has declared it a Public Health Emergency of International Concern.
