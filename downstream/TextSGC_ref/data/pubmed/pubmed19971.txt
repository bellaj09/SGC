Many individuals with Parkinson disease PD suffer from impaired dexterity which impacts activities of daily living and quality of life. Exergaming video game-based training with augmented virtual reality may have value for improving function. The aim of the present pilot study was to comprehensively evaluate the feasibility of a dexterity training program using exergaming in individuals with PD. Exergaming-Based Dexterity Training in Persons With Parkinson Disease: A Pilot Feasibility Study.
