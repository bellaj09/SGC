"Non-immersive video games are currently being used as technological rehabilitation tools for individuals with Parkinsons disease PD. The aim of this feasibility study was to evaluate the effectiveness of the Leap Motion Controller LMC system used with serious games designed for the upper limb UL as well as the levels of satisfaction and compliance among patients in mild-to-moderate stages of the disease." "Leap motion controlled video game-based therapy for upper limb rehabilitation in patients with Parkinsons disease: a feasibility study."
