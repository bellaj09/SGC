Cue-exposure therapy CET has proven its efficacy in treating patients with bulimia nervosa and binge eating disorder who are resistant to standard treatment. Furthermore incorporating virtual reality VR technology is increasingly considered a valid exposure method that may help to increase the efficacy of standard treatments in a variety of eating disorders. Although immersive displays improve the beneficial effects expensive technology is not always necessary. Cue-exposure software for the treatment of bulimia nervosa and binge eating disorder.
