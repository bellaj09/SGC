Advances in the development of information and communication technologies have facilitated social and sexual interrelationships thanks to the websites and apps created to this end. However these resources can also encourage sexual contacts without appropriate preventive measures in relation to HIV and other sexually transmitted infections STIs. How can users be helped to benefit from the advantages of these apps while keeping in mind those preventive measures? Prevention of HIV and Other Sexually Transmitted Infections by Geofencing and Contextualized Messages With a Gamified App UBESAFE: Design and Creation Study.
