Early detection of adverse drug events ADEs from electronic health records is an important challenging task to support pharmacovigilance and drug safety surveillance. A well-known challenge to use clinical text for detection of ADEs is that much of the detailed information is documented in a narrative manner. Clinical natural language processing NLP is the key technology to extract information from unstructured clinical text. MADEx: A System for Detecting Medications Adverse Drug Events and Their Relations from Clinical Notes.
