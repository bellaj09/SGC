Electronic health eHealth is the use of information and communication technology in the context of health care and health research. Recently there has been a rise in the number of eHealth modalities and the frequency with which they are used to deliver technology-assisted self-management interventions for people living with chronic pain. However there has been little or no research directly comparing these eHealth modalities. An Evaluation of the Effectiveness of the Modalities Used to Deliver Electronic Health Interventions for Chronic Pain: Systematic Review With Network Meta-Analysis.
