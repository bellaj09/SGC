The main objectives of this study were to establish expert validity a convincing realistic representation of colonoscopy according to experts and construct validity the ability to discriminate between different levels of expertise of the Simbionix GI Mentor II virtual reality VR simulator for colonoscopy tasks and to assess the didactic value of the simulator as judged by experts. Expert and construct validity of the Simbionix GI Mentor II endoscopy simulator for colonoscopy.
