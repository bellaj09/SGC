"Slip on a set of virtual reality VR goggles and connected gloves and you are transported to another world. For the entertainment industry that kind of immersive VR may mean shoot-em-up games where players have to blast blood-thirsty aliens or other bad guys to save the day. For the military it may be simulations for pilot instruction or soldier training. And for a growing number of researchers-and now companies-the focus is on using VR systems to help patients deal with pain." Virtual Reality Is Taking the Hurt Out of Pain.
