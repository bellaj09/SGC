"Despite high relapse rate among methamphetamine MA abusers there still have been little empirical data to date detecting the risk factors related to craving and relapse from the perspective of MA abusers. Therefore the aim of this study was to use qualitative research methods exploring the strengths weaknesses opportunities and threat factors that could facilitate or impede individuals abstinence in a real-life context from the perspectives of MA abusers." Strengths weaknesses opportunities and threats SWOT analysis of reinitiation into methamphetamine abusers: qualitative findings from an exploration of methamphetamine abusers in Shanghai China.
