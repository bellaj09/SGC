Several approaches to the treatment of bulimia nervosa have proved effective including cognitive-behavioral therapy; however not all patients improve. It is therefore necessary to explore the possibilities of increasing the efficacy of such treatments. One way to attempt this is to incorporate new technologies. This review explores the possibility of developing a new empirically validated procedure for the treatment of bulimia nervosa patients that involves cue exposure via virtual reality. VR cue-exposure treatment for bulimia nervosa.
