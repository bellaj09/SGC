Over 40000 cases of coronavirus CoV disease 2019 COVID-19 have been confirmed in China. The causative agent 2019 novel CoV 2019-nCoV has spread rapidly to more than 25 countries worldwide. Human-to-human transmission has accounted for most of the infections outside Wuhan. Most studies to date on COVID-19 have focused on disease etiology and the genomics of 2019-nCoV with few reports on the epidemiologic and clinical characteristics of infected patients. Epidemiologic and Clinical Characteristics of 26 Cases of COVID-19 Arising from Patient-to-Patient Transmission in Liaocheng China.
