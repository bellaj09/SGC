In December 2019 coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 emerged in Wuhan China. The number of affected pregnant women is increasing but scarce information is available about the clinical features of COVID-19 in pregnancy. This study aimed to clarify the clinical features and obstetric and neonatal outcomes of pregnant patients with COVID-19. Clinical features and obstetric and neonatal outcomes of pregnant patients with COVID-19 in Wuhan China: a retrospective single-centre descriptive study.
