"There are limited case series reporting the impact on women affected by coronavirus during pregnancy. In women affected by severe acute respiratory syndrome SARS and Middle East respiratory syndrome MERS the case fatality rate appears higher in those affected in pregnancy compared with non-pregnant women. We conducted a rapid review to guide health policy and management of women affected by COVID-19 during pregnancy which was used to develop the Royal College of Obstetricians and Gynaecologists RCOG guidelines on COVID-19 infection in pregnancy." Coronavirus in pregnancy and delivery: rapid review.
