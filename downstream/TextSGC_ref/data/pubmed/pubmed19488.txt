"This article presents a unique strategy for improving didactic learning and clinical skill while simultaneously fostering interprofessional collaboration and communication. Senior-level nursing students collaborated with students enrolled in the Department of Interactive Media Studies to design a virtual reality simulation based upon disaster management and triage techniques. Collaborative creation of the simulation proved to be a strategy for enhancing students knowledge of and skill in disaster management and triage while impacting attitudes about interprofessional communication and teamwork." Fostering Learning Through Interprofessional Virtual Reality Simulation Development.
