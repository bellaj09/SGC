The "severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2" SARS-CoV-2 has spread over the four continents causing the respiratory manifestations of Coronavirus disease-19 COVID-19 and satisfying the epidemiological criteria for a pandemic 1. As of April 1 2020 more than one million COVID-19 positive cases have been identified and more than 54000 deaths have occurred worldwide 2. In Italy 110574 positive cases 49285 hospitalized patients and 13155 deaths out of a population of 60359546 inhabitants have been reported respectively 3. The highest number of deaths occurred in the northern Italian regions i.e. Lombardy Emilia-Romagna Veneto and Piedmont 3.
