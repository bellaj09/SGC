Different non-pharmacological techniques including hypnosis and virtual reality VR are currently used as complementary tools in the treatment of anxiety acute and chronic pain. A new technique called virtual reality hypnosis VRH which encompasses a combination of both tools is regularly used although its benefits and underlying mechanisms remain unknown to date. With the goal to improve our understanding of VRH combination effects it is necessary to conduct randomised and controlled research trials in order to understand their clinical interest and potential benefits. Can hypnosis and virtual reality reduce anxiety pain and fatigue among patients who undergo cardiac surgery: a randomised controlled trial.
