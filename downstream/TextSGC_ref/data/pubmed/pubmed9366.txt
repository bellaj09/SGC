"Early diagnosis of Alzheimers disease AD and its onset in subjects affected by mild cognitive impairment MCI based on structural MRI features is one of the most important open issues in neuroimaging. Accordingly a scientific challenge has been promoted on the international Kaggle platform to assess the performance of different classification methods for prediction of MCI and its conversion to AD." "Deep learning reveals Alzheimers disease onset in MCI subjects: Results from an international challenge."
