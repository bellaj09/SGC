Layperson cardiopulmonary resuscitation CPR is a key aspect in the chain of survival after cardiac arrest. New low-cost easily accessible training methods such as virtual reality VR training with a smartphone application may reach broader populations but data on CPR performance are scarce. Rationale and design of the Lowlands Saves Lives trial: a randomised trial to compare CPR quality and long-term attitude towards CPR performance between face-to-face and virtual reality training with the Lifesaver VR app.
