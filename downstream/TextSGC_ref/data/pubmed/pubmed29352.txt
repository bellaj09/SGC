Seven autopsy patients with amyotrophic lateral sclerosis ALS in totally locked-in state TLS were examined neuropathologically. The patients were composed of 4 males and 3 females and 3 with familial 1 sporadic but with mutation in SOD1 gene and 3 sporadic patients with unremarkable gene mutation. The brains weighed 715 783 1019 1050 1170 1190 or 1233 g. The tegmentum of the brain stem was markedly degenerated in every patient and the tracts relating to the somatic sensory and auditory were involved in the lesions. Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis in totally locked-in state.
