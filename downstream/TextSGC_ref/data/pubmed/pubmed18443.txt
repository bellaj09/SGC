The experiment presented is the result of a partnership between the Paris-Nanterre University CLIPSYD laboratory EA 4430 and the CSAPA Imagine Simone Veil Hospital GH Eaubonne-Montmorency. Virtual reality immersion exposure for pathological gamblers is beneficial in the context of cognitive and behavioral interventions. It can be used to evoke the desire to play and to have access to automatic thoughts and mistaken beliefs of the players when they find themselves in a gambling context. Exposure is used until the urge to play diminishes and disappears which is the process of desensitization or extinction. Feasibility study of therapeutic exposure in virtual reality for pathological gamblers.
