Augmented and Virtual Reality approaches are getting more and more advanced and consequently their use in various real world areas is increasing. Medicine is one of the fields in which more practical applications are surfacing mainly approaches that enable new forms of visualization of data obtained from real patients. Our work focuses on providing a new simple practical and efficient way to visualize the brain of a patient both in an Augmented Reality and in a Virtual Reality approach through a smartphone application. Visualizing the brain on a mixed reality smartphone application.
