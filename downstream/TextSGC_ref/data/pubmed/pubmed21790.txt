Virtual reality environments VREs allow for immersion in speaking environments that mimic real-life interactions while maintaining researcher control. VREs have been used successfully to engender arousal in other disorders. The purpose of this study was to investigate the utility of virtual reality environments to examine physiological reactivity and subjective ratings of distress in persons who stutter PWS. Utility of virtual reality environments to examine physiological reactivity and subjective distress in adults who stutter.
