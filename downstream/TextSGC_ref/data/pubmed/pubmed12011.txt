Cancer will replace cardiovascular diseases as the most frequent cause of death. Therefore the goals of cancer treatment are prevention strategies and early detection by cancer screening and ideal stage therapy. From an oncological point of view complete tumor resection is a significant prognostic factor. Optical coherence tomography OCT and confocal laser microscopy CLM are two techniques that have the potential to complement intraoperative frozen section analysis as in vivo and real-time optical biopsies. Towards an Optical Biopsy during Visceral Surgical Interventions.
