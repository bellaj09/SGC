"Self-assessment is fundamental in surgical training. Accuracy of self-assessment is superior with greater age experience and the use of video playback. Presently there is scarce evidence in the literature regarding predictors for a surgical trainees aptitude for self-assessment. The objective of this study was to investigate whether emotional intelligence or visual-spatial aptitude can predict effective self-assessment among novice surgeons performing laparoscopic appendectomy LA." Emotional Intelligence Predicts Accurate Self-Assessment of Surgical Quality: A Pilot Study.
