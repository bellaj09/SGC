With its epicenter in Wuhan China the COVID-19 outbreak was declared a public health emergency of international concern PHEIC by the World Health Organization WHO. Consequently many countries have implemented flight restrictions to China. China itself has imposed a lockdown of the population of Wuhan as well as the entire Hubei province. However whether these two enormous measures have led to significant changes in the spread of COVID-19 cases remains unclear. The positive impact of lockdown in Wuhan on containing the COVID-19 outbreak in China.
