"The recent coronavirus disease COVID-19 pandemic is taking a toll on the worlds health care infrastructure as well as the social economic and psychological well-being of humanity. Individuals organizations and governments are using social media to communicate with each other on a number of issues relating to the COVID-19 pandemic. Not much is known about the topics being shared on social media platforms relating to COVID-19. Analyzing such information can help policy makers and health care organizations assess the needs of their stakeholders and address them appropriately." Top Concerns of Tweeters During the COVID-19 Pandemic: Infoveillance Study.
