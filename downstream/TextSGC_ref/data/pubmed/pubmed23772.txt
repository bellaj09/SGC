Recent advances in fiber optics and digital technology have dramatically improved the ability of the urologic surgeon to accurately image and document endoscopic and laparoscopic procedures. In addition the development of digital endoscopes has expanded the armamentarium of the endoscopic surgeon greatly. Finally new virtual reality technology has allowed increased visualization during surgery and provides excellent training for surgeons. This article details new imaging and documentation technology currently utilized during urologic procedures. New technology for imaging and documenting urologic procedures.
