During the current rapidly evolving pandemic of COVID-19 infection pregnant women with suspected or confirmed COVID-19 and their newborn infants form a special vulnerable group that needs immediate attention. Unlike other elective medical and surgical problems for which care can be deferred during the pandemic pregnancies and childbirths will continue. Perinatal period poses unique challenges and care of the mother-baby dyads requires special resources for prevention of transmission diagnosis of infection and providing clinical care during labor resuscitation and postnatal period. Perinatal-Neonatal Management of COVID-19 Infection - Guidelines of the Federation of Obstetric and Gynecological Societies of India FOGSI National Neonatology Forum of India NNF and Indian Academy of Pediatrics IAP.
