From 1999 onwards deep brain stimulation DBS has been proposed as an alternative to capsulotomy in refractory cases of obsessive-compulsive disorder OCD. Although rechargeable implantable pulse generators rIPGs have been used extensively in DBS for movement disorders there are no reports on rIPGs in patients with a psychiatric DBS indication and even possible objections to their use. Rechargeable Stimulators in Deep Brain Stimulation for Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder: A Prospective Interventional Cohort Study.
