The coronavirus disease COVID-19 pandemic which began in Wuhan China in December 2019 is rapidly spreading worldwide with over 1.9 million cases as of mid-April 2020. Infoveillance approaches using social media can help characterize disease distribution and public knowledge attitudes and behaviors critical to the early stages of an outbreak. Data Mining and Content Analysis of the Chinese Social Media Platform Weibo During the Early COVID-19 Outbreak: Retrospective Observational Infoveillance Study.
