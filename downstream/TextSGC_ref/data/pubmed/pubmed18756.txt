"The Virtual Environment for Radiotherapy Training VERT system is a recently available tool for radiation therapy education. The majority of research regarding VERT-based education is focused on students with a growing area of research being VERTs role in patient education. Because large differences in educational requirements exist between students and patients focused resources and subsequent evaluations are necessary to provide solid justification for the unique benefits and challenges posed by VERT in a patient education context. This commentary article examines VERTs role in patient education with a focus on salient visual features VERTs ability to address some of the spatial challenges associated with RT patient education and how to combine technology with human care." Radiation therapy patient education using VERT: combination of technology with human care.
