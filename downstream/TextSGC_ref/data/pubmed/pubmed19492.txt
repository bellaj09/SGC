To evaluate a haptic-based simulator MicroVisTouch as an assessment tool for capsulorhexis performance in cataract surgery. The study is a prospective unmasked nonrandomized dual academic institution study conducted at the Wilmer Eye Institute at Johns Hopkins Medical Center Baltimore MD USA and King Khaled Eye Specialist Hospital Riyadh Saudi Arabia. The use of a virtual reality surgical simulator for cataract surgical skill assessment with 6 months of intervening operating room experience.
