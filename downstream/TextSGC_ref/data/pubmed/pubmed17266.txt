"To compare dental students perceptions of their learning approaches between different years of a problem-based learning PBL programme. The hypothesis was that in a comparison between senior and junior students the senior students would perceive themselves as having a higher level of deep learning approach and a lower level of surface learning approach than junior students would. This hypothesis was based on the fact that senior students have longer experience of a student-centred educational context which is supposed to underpin student learning." "Dental students perception of their approaches to learning in a PBL programme."
