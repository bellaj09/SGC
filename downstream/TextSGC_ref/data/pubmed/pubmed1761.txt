Current events with the recent COVID-19 outbreak are necessitating steep learning curves for the NHS workforce. Ultrasound although not used in the diagnosis of COVID-19 may be utilised by practitioners at the point of care POC or on the intensive care units ITUs where rapid assessment of the lung condition may be required. The aim of this article was to review current literature surrounding the use of lung ultrasound in relation to COVID-19 and provide Sonographers with a quick and digestible reference guide for lung pathologies. Point of care and intensive care lung ultrasound: A reference guide for practitioners during COVID-19.
