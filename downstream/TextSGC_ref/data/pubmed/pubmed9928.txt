Background: Researchers have contributed to the definition of SoTL; however the literature is not conclusive on its definitions and attributes. Therefore this study was an attempt to precisely define SoTL by its attributes antecedents and consequences. Methods: The Walker and Avant 2011 concept analysis method was used. Results: The 9 emerged attributes are: Committed engagement in action Critique-based Disciplinary Context-oriented Critical enquiry process Continuous deep reflection Dynamic process Shared publicly and Learning focused. Conclusion: This study helps promote understanding and application of SoTL. Exploring the concept of scholarship of teaching and learning SoTL: Concept analysis.
