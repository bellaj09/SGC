The COVID-19 pandemic and the desire to "flatten the curve" of transmission have significantly affected the way providers care for patients. Female Pelvic Medicine and Reconstructive Surgeons FPMRS must provide high quality of care through remote access such as telemedicine. No clear guidelines exist on the use of telemedicine in FPMRS. Using expedited literature review methodology we provide guidance regarding management of common outpatient urogynecology scenarios during the pandemic. A guide for urogynecologic patient care utilizing telemedicine during the COVID-19 pandemic: review of existing evidence.
