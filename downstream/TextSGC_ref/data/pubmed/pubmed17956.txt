The internal mammary vessels IMVs are increasingly the recipients for free flap breast reconstruction FFBR. Access traditionally entails removing a segment of the third costal cartilage. Despite excellent exposure some authors have reported localized tenderness as well as a thoracic contour deformity. We introduced the "total rib preservation" technique for IMV exposure after specific request by a patient and have used it for all subsequent reconstructions. Tips for successful microvascular abdominal flap breast reconstruction utilizing the "total rib preservation" technique for internal mammary vessel exposure.
