On March 11 2020 the World Health Organization WHO declared the novel coronavirus disease COVID-19 a global pandemic. Starting in December 2019 from China the first cases were officially announced on February 19 in Qom city Iran. As of April 3 2020 206 countries have reported a total of 932166 cases with 46764 deaths. Along with China USA Italy Spain and Germany Iran has been suffering the hardest burden of COVID-19 outbreak. Worse still countries like Iran are struggling with the double burden of political sanctions to provide lifesaving medical equipment and medicines to combat the emergency. COVID-19 Pandemic and Comparative Health Policy Learning in Iran.
