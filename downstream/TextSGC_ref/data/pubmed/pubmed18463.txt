Tai Chi TC is a Chinese mind-body exercise with proven physical and psychological benefits. A modified TC via virtual reality VR may be suitable for the elderly owing to the immediate guidance and feedback regarding movement accuracy. This study explored the cognitive and physical effects of a VR-based TC VRTC exercise program on older adults with cognitive impairment CI. The Effectiveness of a Virtual Reality-Based Tai Chi Exercise on Cognitive and Physical Function in Older Adults with Cognitive Impairment.
