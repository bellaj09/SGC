"A foundation of care within a Patient-centered Medical Home PCMH is respect for patients values and preferences. Shared decision making SDM involves a set of principles and approaches to working with patients that integrates medical information and data with the preferences values and support systems of individual patients facing medical decisions. The value of SDM is increasingly evidenced by the incorporation of principles of SDM into the definitions of patient-centered care and PCMHs accountable care organizations and the language of the Health Reform Act of 2010." Shared decision making: skill acquisition for year III medical students.
