With an increasing percentage of colorectal resections performed laparoscopically nowadays there is more emphasis on training before the job on operative skills including the comprehension of specific laparoscopic surgical anatomy. As integration of technical skills with correct interpretation of the anatomical image must be incorporated in laparoscopic training a human specimen training model with special emphasis on surgical anatomy was developed. Training in laparoscopic colorectal surgery: a new educational model using specially embalmed human anatomical specimen.
