"Post-natal Depression PND is a depressive disorder that causes significant distress or impairment on different levels in the individuals life and their families. There is already evidence of the efficacy of psychological treatments for PND. We conducted a narrative review and researched the literature for identifying systematic reviews and studies for the best psychological treatments of PND and examined what parameters made those treatments successful." Cognitive-Behavioural therapy and interpersonal psychotherapy for the treatment of post-natal depression: a narrative review.
