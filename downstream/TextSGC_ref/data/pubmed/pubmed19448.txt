"Immersive virtual reality iVR allows seamless interaction with simulated environments and is becoming an established tool in clinical research. It is unclear whether iVR is acceptable to people with Alzheimers disease AD dementia or useful in their care. We explore whether iVR is a viable research tool that may aid the detection and treatment of AD." "Use of Immersive Virtual Reality in the Assessment and Treatment of Alzheimers Disease: A Systematic Review."
