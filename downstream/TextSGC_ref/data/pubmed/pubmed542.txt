In late 2019 an outbreak of a new coronavirus named severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 was detected in Wuhan China. A great percentage of patients with this disease developed symptoms of dry cough malaise and a high fever. During this time several patients requiring assessment and treatment of endodontic emergencies were directed to the School and Hospital of Stomatology at Wuhan University Wuhan China. We examined the characteristics of these patients. Characteristics of Endodontic Emergencies during Coronavirus Disease 2019 Outbreak in Wuhan.
