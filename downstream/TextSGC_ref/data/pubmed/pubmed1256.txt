Since the outbreak of coronavirus disease COVID-19 in Wuhan in December 2019 by March 10 2020 a total of 80932 confirmed cases have been reported in China. Two consecutively negative RT-PCR test results in respiratory tract specimens is required for the evaluation of discharge from hospital and oropharyngeal swabs were the most common sample. However false negative results occurred in the late stage of hospitalization and avoiding false negative result is critical essential. Comparison of nasopharyngeal and oropharyngeal swabs for SARS-CoV-2 detection in 353 patients received tests with both specimens simultaneously.
