The Virtual Basic Laparoscopic Skill Trainer VBLaST  is a virtual-reality alternative for the Foundations of Laparoscopic Surgery FLS trainer system. In this work we present the results from our face validation expriments for the VBLaST. We have shown that the VBLaST simulator has excellent face validity in portraying the FLS tasks as well as interface and movements of the tool. Face validation of the virtual basic laparoscopic skill trainer VBLaST.
