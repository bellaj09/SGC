Artificial intelligence in medicine has made dramatic progress in recent years. However much of this progress is seemingly scattered lacking a cohesive structure for the discerning observer. In this article we will provide an up-to-date review of artificial intelligence in medicine with a specific focus on its application to radiology pathology ophthalmology and dermatology. We will discuss a range of selected papers that illustrate the potential uses of artificial intelligence in a technologically advanced future. Artificial Intelligence in Medicine: Where Are We Now?
