"This study aimed to investigate the effectiveness of using a virtual reality VR casino environment in cue exposure therapy CET for gambling. The main objective of this study was to assess the ability of five VR casino cues to elicit subjective reactions and physiological responses that can be used within the CET paradigm. A second objective was to analyze changes in participants urge to gamble after repeated exposure to a VR casino program and relaxation training."
