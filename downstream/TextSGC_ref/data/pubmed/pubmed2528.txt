Emerging infectious disease outbreaks such as the present coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 pandemic often have a psychological impact on the well-being of the general population including survivors and caregivers. Our study aimed to synthesise extant literature regarding the combined psychological responses and coping methods used by the general population in past outbreaks. Narrative synthesis of psychological and coping responses towards emerging infectious disease outbreaks in the general population: practical considerations for the COVID-19 pandemic.
