One prominent symptom in addiction disorders is the strong desire to consume a particular substance or to display a certain behaviour craving. Especially the strong association between craving and the probability of relapse emphasises the importance of craving in the therapeutic process. Neuroimaging studies have shown that craving is associated with increased responses predominantly in fronto-striatal areas. Modulation of Craving Related Brain Responses Using Real-Time fMRI in Patients with Alcohol Use Disorder.
