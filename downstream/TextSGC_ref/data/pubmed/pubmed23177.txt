Two case examples of female dental patients aged 56 and 24 years who met the criteria for dental phobia according to the Phobia Checklist illustrate the use of VRET in the dental setting. VRET that is used as a psychological treatment for dental fear and dental phobia can potentially be given by a non-specialist for example dental assistant thereby making it a cost-effective therapy for the treatment of dental phobia. Clinical relevance: This article is the first of its kind to demonstrate Virtual Reality Exposure Therapy VRET in the treatment of dental anxiety.
