Passing the American Board of Anesthesiology ABA basic examination is required to progress through anesthesiology training in USA. Failing the test may be related to medical knowledge gaps presence of negative psychosocial factors and/or individual approaches to learning. This article describes the experience of development and implementation of a multifaceted remediation program MRP in residents who failed the ABA basic test. Multifaceted remediation program: experience of a residency program to rescue residents who failed the American Board of Anesthesiology basic examination.
