In December 2019 unexplained pneumonia cases were initially reported in Wuhan China. The pathogen a novel coronavirus named severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 was isolated from lower respiratory tract samples of infected patients and the resultant disease was termed as COVID-19 Coronavirus Disease 20191 . By Feb 15 COVID-19 has rapidly spread throughout China and across the world until a pandemic condition was announced by March 112 . Cutaneous manifestations in COVID-19: a first perspective.
