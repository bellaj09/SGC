Surgical approaches to the orbit are challenging and require combined multispecialist skills. Considering its increasing relevance in neurosurgical practice keyhole surgery could be also applied to this field. However mastering a minimally invasive approach necessitates an extended learning curve. For this reason virtual reality VR can be effectively used for planning and training in this demanding surgical technique. Mini Fronto-Orbital Approach: "Window Opening" Towards the Superomedial Orbit - A Virtual Reality-Planned Anatomic Study.
