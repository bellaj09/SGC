After the video tutorials created a few months ago and published in the Journal of Adolescent and Young Adult Oncology Milan\s Youth Project young cancer patients decided to record another video: a "special issue" dedicated to the coronavirus disease 2019 pandemic. The video addresses critical issues of the social isolation imposed by the lockdown and the fear the patients face in addition to their actual worries related to the disease. When many people across the world are forced to social distance for the imposed lockdown the positive attitude and the advice of young cancer patients who experience social isolation regularly may be of great support and a fine example. A New Video Tumorial by Young Cancer Patients Dedicated to COVID-19 Pandemic and Lockdown Re: J Adolesc Young Adult Oncol. Epub ahead of print; DOI: 10.1089/jayao.2020.0008.
