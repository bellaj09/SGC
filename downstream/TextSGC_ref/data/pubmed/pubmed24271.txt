Embedded in the Collaborative Research Center "Fear Anxiety Anxiety Disorders" CRC-TRR58 this bicentric clinical study aims at identifying biobehavioral markers of treatment non-response by applying machine learning methodology with an external cross-validation protocol. We hypothesize that a priori prediction of treatment non-response is possible in a second independent sample based on multimodal markers. Theranostic markers for personalized therapy of spider phobia: Methods of a bicentric external cross-validation machine learning approach.
