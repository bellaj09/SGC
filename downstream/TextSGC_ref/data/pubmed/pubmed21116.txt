Worldwide there has been a marked increase in stress and anxiety also among patients with traumatic brain injury TBI. Access to psychology services is limited with some estimates suggesting that over 50% of sufferers are not accessing the existing services available to them for reasons such as inconvenience embarrassment or stigmatization concerns around mental health. Health service providers have increasingly been turning to drug-free therapies such as mindfulness programs as complementary treatments.
