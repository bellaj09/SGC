Despite the rapidly increasing attention being given to Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome Coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 infection more commonly known as coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 the relationship between cardiovascular disease and COVID-19 has not been fully described.Methods\u2004and\u2004Results:A systematic review was undertaken to summarize the important aspects of COVID-19 for cardiologists.
