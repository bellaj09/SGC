Since January 2020 3 months ago when the pathogen causing the Coronavirus-disease was identified in humans the literature on COVID-19 has grown exponentially to over 4000 publications. There is the need to provide an update for each single medical discipline including neurosurgery to be used by single professionals or to be distributed through the neurosurgical community and to be used by governments in designing new scenario of care. COVID-19 and Neurosurgery. Literature and Neurosurgical Societies Recommendations Update.
