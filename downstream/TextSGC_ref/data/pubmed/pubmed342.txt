In December 2019 severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 a novel coronavirus emerged in Wuhan China. Since then the city of Wuhan has taken unprecedented measures in response to the outbreak including extended school and workplace closures. We aimed to estimate the effects of physical distancing measures on the progression of the COVID-19 epidemic hoping to provide some insights for the rest of the world. The effect of control strategies to reduce social mixing on outcomes of the COVID-19 epidemic in Wuhan China: a modelling study.
