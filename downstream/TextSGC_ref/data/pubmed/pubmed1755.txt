Between January 24th and March 10th a total of 2370 individuals had contact with the first 30 cases of COVID-19. There were 13 individuals who contracted COVID-19 resulting in a secondary attack rate of 0.55% 95% CI 0.31-0.96. There were 119 household contacts of which 9 individuals developed COVID-19 resulting in a secondary attack rate of 7.56% 95% CI 3.7-14.26. Coronavirus Disease-19: Summary of 2370 Contact Investigations of the First 30 Cases in the Republic of Korea.
