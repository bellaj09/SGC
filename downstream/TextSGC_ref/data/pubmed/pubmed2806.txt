Since the first case of severe acute respiratory syndrome Coronavirus-2 SARS-CoV-2 occurred in Wuhan in December 2019 the virus has spread globally. The World Health Organization declared the virus outbreak a pandemic on March 11 2020. On January 19 2020 a 35-year-old woman who returned from China was confirmed as the first SARS-CoV-2 infected case in Korea. Since then it has spread all over Korea.
