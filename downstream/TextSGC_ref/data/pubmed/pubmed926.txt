The pandemic coronavirus disease-19 COVID-19 has pushed the global healthcare system to a crisis and amounted to a huge economic burden. Different drugs for prophylaxis against COVID-19 including chloroquine CQ or hydroxychloroquine HCQ have been tried. This study was performed to systematically review the role of CQ and HCQ in preventing the spread of COVID-19. A systematic review of the prophylactic role of chloroquine and hydroxychloroquine in coronavirus disease-19 COVID-19.
