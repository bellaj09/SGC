This study compared live instructor-led training in Personal Protective Equipment PPE donning and doffing with video-based instruction. It assessed the difference in performance between i attending one instructor-led training session in donning and doffing PPE one month prior to assessment and ii watching training videos over the month. A RANDOMIZED TRIAL OF INSTRUCTOR-LED TRAINING VERSUS VIDEO LESSON IN TRAINING HEALTH CARE PROVIDERS IN PROPER DONNING AND DOFFING OF PERSONAL PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT.
