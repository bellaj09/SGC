The purpose of this study is to evaluate the predictive value of the amount of fibroglandular tissue FGT and background parenchymal enhancement BPE measured at baseline on breast MRI for breast cancer development and risk of false-positive findings in women at increased risk for breast cancer. Amount of fibroglandular tissue FGT and background parenchymal enhancement BPE in relation to breast cancer risk and false positives in a breast MRI screening program : A retrospective cohort study.
