Understanding risk perceptions of the public is critical for risk communication. In February 2020 the Finnish Institute for Health and Welfare started collecting weekly qualitative data on coronavirus disease COVID-19 risk perception that informs risk communication efforts. The process is based on thematic analysis of emails and social media messages from the public and identifies factors linked to appraisal of risk magnitude which are developed into risk communication recommendations together with health and communication experts. Understanding coronavirus disease COVID-19 risk perceptions among the public to enhance risk communication efforts: a practical approach for outbreaks Finland February 2020.
