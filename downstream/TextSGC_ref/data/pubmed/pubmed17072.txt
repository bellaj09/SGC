Cleft palate is a birth defect characterized by a lack of fusion between structures forming the palate. Causes include a multitude of factors both genetic and environmental. Computed tomography CT is widely used to evaluate morphological features and diagnose head disorders in adult dogs. However there is less data about its use in neonatal dogs. The purpose of this study was to perform CT evaluation of palatal defects in one-day-old puppies and to present a novel approach of 3D modeling in terms of cleft palate assessment. Computed tomographic evaluation of cleft palate in one-day-old puppies.
