A range of public health measures have been implemented to suppress local transmission of coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 in Hong Kong. We examined the effect of these interventions and behavioural changes of the public on the incidence of COVID-19 as well as on influenza virus infections which might share some aspects of transmission dynamics with COVID-19. Impact assessment of non-pharmaceutical interventions against coronavirus disease 2019 and influenza in Hong Kong: an observational study.
