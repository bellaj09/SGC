Reducing childhood morbidity and mortality is challenging particularly in countries with a shortage of qualified health care workers. Lack of trainers makes it difficult to provide the necessary continuing education in pediatrics for postregistration health professionals. Digital education teaching and learning by means of digital technologies has the potential to deliver medical education to a large audience while limiting the number of trainers needed. Digital Health Professions Education in the Field of Pediatrics: Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis by the Digital Health Education Collaboration.
