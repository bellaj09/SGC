Coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 most commonly presents with respiratory symptoms including cough shortness of breath and sore throat. However digestive symptoms also occur in patients with COVID-19 and are often described in outpatients with less severe disease. In this study we sought to describe the clinical characteristics of COVID-19 patients with digestive symptoms and mild disease severity. Digestive Symptoms in COVID-19 Patients With Mild Disease Severity: Clinical Presentation Stool Viral RNA Testing and Outcomes.
