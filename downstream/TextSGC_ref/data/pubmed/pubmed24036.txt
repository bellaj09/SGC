Body image BI disturbances are one of the core symptoms of anorexia nervosa AN. They have been shown to be associated with depression and anxiety at all treatment stages and are a reliable predictor of relapse. Considering the importance for the course of AN direct targeting of BI disturbances is still underemphasized in the treatment of patients with AN. Effectiveness of body image directed interventions in patients with anorexia nervosa: A systematic review.
