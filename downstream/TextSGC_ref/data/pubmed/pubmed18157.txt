"Three studies were conducted to examine the effect of audio on peoples experience in a virtual world. The first study showed that people could distinguish between mono stereo Dolby surround and 3D audio of a wasp. The second study found significant effects for audio techniques on peoples self-reported anxiety presence and spatial perception. The third study found that adding sound to a visual virtual world had a significant effect on peoples experience including heart rate while it found no difference in experience between stereo and 3D audio." The Effect Of 3D Audio And Other Audio Techniques On Virtual Reality Experience.
