The purpose of this study was to ascertain the feasibility and potential effect of a low-cost VR system eMagin Z800 3DVisor used in conjunction with pharmacological analgesia on reducing pain and anxiety in adult burn patients undergoing physiotherapy treatment compared to pharmacologic analgesia alone at a South African hospital. Feasibility and potential effect of a low-cost virtual reality system on reducing pain and anxiety in adult burn injury patients during physiotherapy in a developing country.
