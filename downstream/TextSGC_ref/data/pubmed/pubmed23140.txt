Acute alcohol intoxication selectively impairs executive functioning and prospective memory PM. Much previous researches in this area have used laboratorybased tasks that may not mimic functions that individuals with dysexecutive syndrome have problems with in their everyday life. The present study aimed to assess the effects of a modest dose of alcohol on executive functioning and PM using a virtual reality task and investigate the role of executive planning in PM performance.
