Surgical training methods are evolving with the technological advancements including the application of virtual reality VR and augmented reality. However 28 to 40% of novice residents are not confident in performing a major surgical procedure. VR surgery an immersive VR iVR experience was developed using Oculus Rift and Leap Motion devices Leap Motion Inc San Francisco CA to address this challenge. Our iVR is a multisensory holistic surgical training application that demonstrates a maxillofacial surgical technique the Le Fort I osteotomy. The main objective of the present study was to evaluate the effect of using VR surgery on the self-confidence and knowledge of surgical residents. Effectiveness of Immersive Virtual Reality in Surgical Training-A Randomized Control Trial.
