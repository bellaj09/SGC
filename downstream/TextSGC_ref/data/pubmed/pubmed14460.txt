Retinal fundus image analysis without manual intervention has been rising as an imperative analytical approach for early detection of eye-related diseases such as glaucoma and diabetic retinopathy. For analysis and detection of Glaucoma and some other disease from retinal image there is a significant role of predicting the bounding box coordinates of Optic Disc OD that acts as a Region of Interest ROI. The region of interest localization for glaucoma analysis from retinal fundus image using deep learning.
