Since December 2019 Wuhan China has experienced an outbreak of coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 caused by the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2. Epidemiological and clinical characteristics of patients with COVID-19 have been reported but risk factors for mortality and a detailed clinical course of illness including viral shedding have not been well described. Clinical course and risk factors for mortality of adult inpatients with COVID-19 in Wuhan China: a retrospective cohort study.
