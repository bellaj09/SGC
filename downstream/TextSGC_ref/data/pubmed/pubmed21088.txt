Practice on virtual reality simulators VRSs has been shown to improve surgical performance. However VRSs are expensive and usually housed in surgical skills centers that may be inaccessible at times convenient for surgical trainees to practice. Conversely box trainers BT are inexpensive and can be used anywhere at anytime. This study assesses "take-home" BTs as an alternative to VRS. "Take-home" box trainers are an effective alternative to virtual reality simulators.
