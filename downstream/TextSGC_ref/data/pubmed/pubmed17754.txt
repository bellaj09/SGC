Over the last 2 decades the deep inferior epigastric perforator DIEP flap has gained significant popularity in breast reconstruction. However for some the increased technical difficulties associated with DIEP breast reconstruction can be a discouraging factor in adopting this technique. There has been a trend in the literature to acknowledge an acute learning curve in the adoption of DIEP breast reconstruction but little evidence to support its ongoing relevance to surgeons who gain significant exposure in training. The Deep Inferior Epigastric Perforator Learning Curve in the Current Era.
