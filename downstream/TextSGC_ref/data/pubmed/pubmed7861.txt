Methods available for assessing the learning curve such as a predefined number of procedures or direct mentoring are lacking. Our aim was to describe the use of a statistical method to identify the minimal training length of an experienced sonographer newly trained in deep infiltrating endometriosis DIE mapping by evaluating the learning curve of transvaginal ultrasound TVUS in the preoperative assessment of endometriosis. Applying a statistical method in transvaginal ultrasound training: lessons from the learning curve cumulative summation test LC-CUSUM for endometriosis mapping.
