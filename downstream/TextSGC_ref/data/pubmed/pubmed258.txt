Among surgeons worldwide a concern with the use of minimally invasive techniques has been raised due to a proposed risk of viral transmission of the coronavirus disease of 2019 COVID-19 with the creation of pneumoperitoneum. Due to this proposed concern we sought to collect the available data and evaluate the use of laparoscopy and the risk of COVID-19 transmission. What Is the Appropriate Use of Laparoscopy over Open Procedures in the Current COVID-19 Climate?
