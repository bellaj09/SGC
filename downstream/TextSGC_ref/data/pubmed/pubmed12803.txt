The Award for Distinguished Contributions of Applications of Psychology to Education and Training acknowledges psychologists who contribute to new teaching methods or solutions to learning problems through the use of research findings or evidence-based practices. Particular emphasis is placed on the use of psychological knowledge to improve learning in educational settings including prekindergarten to Grade 12 or in communities. The 2014 recipient is Robert L. Hatcher. He is acknowledged "for his deep and abiding commitment to improving training for psychologists for his leadership in developing practicum competencies and for his vision for competency-based education in psychology." Hatcher\s award citation biography and a selected bibliography are presented. Robert L. Hatcher: Award for Distinguished Contributions of Applications of Psychology to Education and Training.
