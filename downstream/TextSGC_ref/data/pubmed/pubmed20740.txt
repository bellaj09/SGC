"In order to inspire a vision of biotechnology that affirms human dignity and human flourishing the author poses questions about virtual reality and the use of robotics in health care. Using the concept of liturgy and an anthropology of humans as lovers the author explores how virtual reality and robotics in health care shape human moral agents and how such shaping could influence the way we do or do not pursue a posthuman future." A posthuman liturgy? Virtual worlds robotics and human flourishing.
