Adults with traumatic brain injuries TBIs often struggle with prospective memory PM the ability to remember to complete tasks in the future such as taking medicines on a schedule. Metamemory judgments or how well we think we will do at remembering are linked to strategy use and are critical for managing demands of daily living. The current project used an Internet-based virtual reality tool to assess metamemory judgments of PM following TBI. Predicting Remembering: Judgments of Prospective Memory After Traumatic Brain Injury.
