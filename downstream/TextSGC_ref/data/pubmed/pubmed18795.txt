"With the advent of The Youth Justice and Criminal Evidence Act 1999 passed by Parliament in August 1999 vulnerable witnesses can for the first time give evidence to the court with the support of special measures. People with a learning disability fall into the category of vulnerable witnesses and the purpose of this paper is to describe the development of the virtual courtroom a virtual reality VR and multimedia based training platform to prepare this group of people for such an eventuality." The virtual courtroom: a view of justice. Project to prepare witnesses or victims with learning disabilities to give evidence.
