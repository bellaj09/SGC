The pre-operative three-dimensional 3D imaging technique has resulted in a better surgical outcome for patients and has been used as an education and diagnostic tool. However there are no reports concerning the usefulness of the 3D imaging technique in laparoscopic transabdominal pre-peritoneal repair TAPP so the aim of this study was to investigate the usefulness of the 3D imaging technique in laparoscopic TAPP as an educational tool for medical students. Usefulness of virtual three-dimensional image analysis in inguinal hernia as an educational tool.
