One third of the depressed patients are not improved by antidepressant drugs and psychological treatments and there is a need for additional treatments. Repetitive transcranial magnetic stimulation rTMS is being developed towards an alternative in treatment-resistant depression. Deep transcranial stimulation dTMS with the Hesel-coil H-coil is a further development of rTMS aiming to enhance the effect by getting the magnetic pulses to penetrate deeper into the brain. Effects of Hesel-coil deep transcranial magnetic stimulation for depression - a systematic review.
