The coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 pandemic created a rapid and unprecedented shift in our medical system. Medical providers teams and organizations have needed to shift their visits away from face-to-face visits and toward telehealth both by phone and through video. Palliative care teams who practice in the community setting are faced with a difficult task: How do we actively triage the most urgent visits while keeping our vulnerable patients safe from the pandemic? Rapid De-Escalation and Triaging Patients in Community-Based Palliative Care.
