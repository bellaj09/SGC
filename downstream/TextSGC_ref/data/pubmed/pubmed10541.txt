"Students epistemic beliefs may vary in different domains; therefore it may be beneficial for medical educators to better understand medical students epistemic beliefs regarding medicine. Understanding how medical students are aware of medical knowledge and how they learn medicine is a critical issue of medical education. The main purposes of this study were to investigate medical students epistemic beliefs relating to medical knowledge and to examine their relationships with students approaches to learning medicine." Exploring the relationships between epistemic beliefs about medicine and approaches to learning medicine: a structural equation modeling analysis.
