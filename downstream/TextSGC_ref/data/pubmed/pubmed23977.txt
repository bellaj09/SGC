Cerebral palsy CP is a permanent disorder of movement muscle tone or posture that is caused by damage to the immature and developing brain. Research has shown that Virtual Reality VR technology can be used in rehabilitation to support the acquisition of motor skills and the achievement of functional tasks. The aim of this study was to explore for improvements in the performance of individuals with CP with practice in the use of a virtual game on a mobile phone and to compare their performance with that of the control group. Motor performance of individuals with cerebral palsy in a virtual game using a mobile phone.
