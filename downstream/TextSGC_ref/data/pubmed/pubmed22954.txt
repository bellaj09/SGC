The workflow digital to aid the treatment of dentofacial deformities is a reality. Associated with the virtual planning the creation of surgical guides assists the performance of osteotomies and bone positioning increasing the accuracy of surgical outcomes. This study aims to present a new method of surgical guide for genioplasty based on the selected osteosynthesis plate. A New Proposal for Three-Dimensional Positioning of the Chin Using a Single Computer-Aided Design/Computer-Aided Manufacturing Surgical Guide.
