The coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 has swept the globe and is causing significant morbidity and mortality. Given that the virus is transmitted via droplets open airway procedures such as bronchoscopy pose a significant risk to health care workers HCW. The goal of this guideline was to examine the current evidence on the role of bronchoscopy during the COVID-19 pandemic and the optimal protection of patients and HCW. The Use of Bronchoscopy during the COVID-19 Pandemic: CHEST/AABIP Guideline and Expert Panel Report.
