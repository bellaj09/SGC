The unprecedented outbreak of 2019-nCoV pneumonia infection in Wuhan City caused global concern the outflowing population from Wuhan was believed to be a main reason for the rapid and large-scale spread of the disease so the government implemented a city closure measure to prevent its transmission considering the large amount of travelling before the Chinese New Year. Population movement city closure in Wuhan and geographical expansion of the 2019-nCoV pneumonia infection in China in January 2020.
