In early 2020 the World Health Organization declared the outbreak of the disease COVID-19 caused by a new variant of coronavirus 2019-nCoV as a global pandemic. The government of India ordered a nationwide lockdown for 21 days limiting movement of people as a preventive measure. This survey was designed and conducted during the lockdown period to assess its effect on ophthalmic practice and patient care in India. Effect of COVID-19 related lockdown on ophthalmic practice and patient care in India: Results of a survey.
