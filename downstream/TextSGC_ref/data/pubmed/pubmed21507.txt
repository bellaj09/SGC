"Play is an essential part of childrens lives. Children with physical disabilities experience difficulties in play especially those with severe physical disabilities. With the progress of innovative technology the possibilities to support play are increasing. The purpose of this literature study is to gain insight into the aims control options and commercial availability of information and communication technology ICT and robots to support play especially play for the sake of play in children with severe physical disabilities." Robots and ICT to support play in children with severe physical disabilities: a systematic review.
