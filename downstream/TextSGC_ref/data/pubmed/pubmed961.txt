"At the onset of the coronavirus outbreak the World Health Organizations WHO Health Emergencies Learning and Capacity Development Unit together with the WHOs health technical lead on coronaviruses developed a massive open online course within 3 weeks as part of the global response to the emergency. The introductory coronavirus disease COVID19 course was launched on January 26 2020 on the health emergencies learning platform OpenWHO.org."
