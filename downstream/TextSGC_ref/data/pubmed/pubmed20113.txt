"In recent years virtual reality VR has been tested as a therapeutic tool in neurorehabilitation research. However the impact effectiveness of VR technology on for Parkinsons Disease PD patients is still remains controversial unclear. In order to provide a more scientific basis for rehabilitation of PD patients modality we conducted a systematic review of VR rehabilitation training for PD patients and focused on the improvement of gait and balance." "Effects of virtual reality rehabilitation training on gait and balance in patients with Parkinsons disease: A systematic review."
