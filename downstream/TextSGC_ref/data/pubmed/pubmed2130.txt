The novel coronavirus COVID-19 is responsible for more fatalities than the SARS coronavirus despite being in the initial stage of a global pandemic. The first suspected case in the Philippines was investigated on January 22 2020 and 633 suspected cases were reported as of March 1. We describe the clinical and epidemiological aspects of the first two confirmed COVID-19 cases in the Philippines both admitted to the national infectious disease referral hospital in Manila. First COVID-19 infections in the Philippines: a case report.
