"Freezing of gait is a debilitating symptom affecting many patients with Parkinsons disease PD causing severe immobility and decreased quality of life. Turning is known to be the most common trigger for freezing and also causes the highest rates of falls. However the pathophysiological basis for these effects is not well understood." "Brain activation underlying turning in Parkinsons disease patients with and without freezing of gait: a virtual reality fMRI study."
