The essential feature of post-traumatic stress disorder PTSD is the development of characteristic symptoms following exposure to one or more traumatic events. According to evidence-based intervention guidelines and empirical evidence one of the most extensively researched and validated treatments for PTSD is prolonged exposure to traumatic events; however exposure therapy can present some limitations. Virtual reality VR can help to improve prolonged exposure because it creates fictitious safe and controllable situations that can enhance emotional engagement and acceptance. Virtual reality exposure-based therapy for the treatment of post-traumatic stress disorder: a review of its efficacy the adequacy of the treatment protocol and its acceptability.
