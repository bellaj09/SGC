There is as yet no consensual definition of "connected health". In general the term refers to the growing use of technology and in particular mobile technology in medicine. Over the past 10 years there have been an increasing number of published reports on the wide-ranging and heterogeneous fields involving the application of technology in medicine ranging from telemedicine to tools to improve patients\ evaluation and monitoring by physicians as well as a multitude of patient-centered applications. They also represent promising tools in the field of clinical research. This report is a review of the importance of using this technology in the management of multiple sclerosis patients.
