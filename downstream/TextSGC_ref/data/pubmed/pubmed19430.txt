Even in the early phases of psychotic spectrum illnesses such as schizophrenia patients can experience cognitive decline or deficits prior to the onset of psychotic symptoms such as delusions and hallucinations. In this systematic review we assessed which verbal memory assessments are most widely used in first-episode psychosis and may be applied via digital technologies smartphone applications etc. for use in early detection. Verbal memory measurement towards digital perspectives in first-episode psychosis: A review.
