Closed loop CL automated insulin delivery systems are demonstrated to be safe and effective in regulating glucose levels and reducing cognitive burden in people with type 1 diabetes T1D. However given the limited market options and the do-it-yourself nature of most systems it can be difficult for potential users to shape their expectations fitting them into daily lives and management routines. As such we examined the potential feasibility of a virtual reality VR intervention. Exposure to Closed Loop Barriers Using Virtual Reality.
