"Postural instability is one of the most disabling features in Parkinsons disease PD and often leads to falls that reduce mobility and functional capacity. The objectives of this study were to analyse the limit of stability LOS and influence of the manipulation of visual somatosensorial and visual-vestibular information on postural control in patients with PD and healthy subjects." "Changes in postural control in patients with Parkinsons disease: a posturographic study."
