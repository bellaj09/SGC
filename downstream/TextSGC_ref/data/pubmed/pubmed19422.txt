The French National Authority for Health requires that otolaryngology students demonstrate competency before they are allowed to interact with human patients or volunteers. However resources are often limited to provide extensive training for students. We developed a virtual reality VR system that simulates the clinical environment to train ear-nose-throat ENT residents. VR simulators have been developed and successfully used for medical training. Using virtual reality in audiological training: Our experience in 22 otolaryngology residents.
