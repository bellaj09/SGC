Few data are available describing the benefits of initiating fundamentals of laparoscopic surgery FLS training during medical school. We hypothesized that an intense 1-month surgical skills elective that included FLS task training for fourth-year medical students MS4s would result in performance levels indistinguishable from graduating chief residents PGY5 who had received clinical skill training and access to self-guided FLS curriculum. Value of fundamentals of laparoscopic surgery training in a fourth-year medical school advanced surgical skills elective.
