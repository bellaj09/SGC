To evaluate whether people with Duchenne muscular dystrophy DMD practicing a task in a virtual environment could improve performance given a similar task in a real environment as well as distinguishing whether there is transference between performing the practice in virtual environment and then a real environment and vice versa. Achievement of Virtual and Real Objects Using a Short-Term Motor Learning Protocol in People with Duchenne Muscular Dystrophy: A Crossover Randomized Controlled Trial.
