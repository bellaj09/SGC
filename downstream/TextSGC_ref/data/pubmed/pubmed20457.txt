"The aim of this study was to investigate the transfer of skills between various levels of force feedback FFB using the Simodont dental trainer Moog Nieuw-Vennep the Netherlands. Students practiced a manual dexterity exercise in a virtual reality environment at a standard level of FFB and then were tested at the standard and an altered level of FFB. In addition the students satisfaction with the training exercise was evaluated." The Effect of Variations in Force Feedback in a Virtual Reality Environment on the Performance and Satisfaction of Dental Students.
