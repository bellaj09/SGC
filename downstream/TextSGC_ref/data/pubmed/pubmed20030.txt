Consumer virtual reality VR devices are becoming more prevalent in the market but cybersickness induced by VR devices limits their potential application and promotion. Acustimulation has been found effective in reducing cybersickness symptoms. However in previous forms the more effective way of acustimulation is either intrusive or electrical which is hard to be applied to daily VR use. Effect of economically friendly acustimulation approach against cybersickness in video-watching tasks using consumer virtual reality devices.
