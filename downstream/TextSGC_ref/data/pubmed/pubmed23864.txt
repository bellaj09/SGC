The European Commission identified active and healthy ageing as a societal challenge common to all European countries and an area which presents considerable potential for Europe to lead the world in providing innovative responses to this challenge http://ec.europa.eu/active-healthy-ageing. To tackle the challenge of an ageing population the European Commission launched the European Innovation Partnership EIP on Active and Healthy Ageing. What can cyberpsychology offer to this process? After presenting the main features of cyberpsychology this paper identifies in patient engagement and positive technologies the key assets that will allow the technological innovations constantly being developed to provide greater help and care in enabling elderly people to live more normal happier fulfilling lives. The quest for active and healthy ageing: what cyberpsychology can offer.
