Simulation training remains a hot topic in surgical education and in particular arthroscopic surgery. There are many challenges associated with simulation training including cost validity and reliability of simulator models as well as the ability to transfer knowledge and/or skills learned to the actual operating room among others. The pace at which simulator technology is expanding is rapid and requires educators to evaluate each new model with respect to face and construct validity as well as educational value and user-friendliness.
