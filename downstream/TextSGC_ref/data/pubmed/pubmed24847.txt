Aging is generally considered to be related to physical and cognitive decline. This is especially prominent in the frontal and parietal lobes underlying executive functions and spatial memory respectively. This process could be successfully mitigated in certain ways such as through the practice of aerobic sports. With regard to this dancing integrates physical exercise with music and involves retrieval of complex sequences of steps and movements creating choreographies. Shall We Dance? Dancing Modulates Executive Functions and Spatial Memory.
