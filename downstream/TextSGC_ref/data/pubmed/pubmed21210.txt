Virtual reality VR and physical model PM simulators differ in terms of whether the trainee is manipulating actual 3-dimensional objects PM or computer-generated 3-dimensional objects VR. Much like video games VG VR simulators utilize computer-generated graphics. These differences may have profound effects on the utility of VR and PM training platforms. In this study we aimed to determine whether a relationship exists between VR PM and VG platforms. Virtual reality simulators: valuable surgical skills trainers or video games?
