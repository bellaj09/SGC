GPs at the Deep End first started in Scotland and brought together Scottish general practitioners GPs working in the 100 most deprived practices in the country. The group continues to provide peer support advocacy training and research opportunities to learn more about general practice in disadvantaged areas. In 2016 Canberra GPs came together to form a local Deep End group supported by the Scottish initiators. GPs at the Deep End: Identifying and addressing social disadvantage wherever it lies.
