Dental treatments often cause pain and anxiety in patients. Virtual reality VR is a novel procedure that can provide distraction during dental procedures or prepare patients to receive such type of treatments. This meta-analysis is the first to gather evidence on the effectiveness of VR on the reduction of pain P and dental anxiety DA in patients undergoing dental treatment regardless of age. Use of Virtual Reality for the Management of Anxiety and Pain in Dental Treatments: Systematic Review and Meta-Analysis.
