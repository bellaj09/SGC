During the outbreak of coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 consistent and considerable differences in disease severity and mortality rate of patients treated in Hubei province compared to those in other parts of China has been observed. We sought to compare the clinical characteristics and outcomes of patients being treated inside and outside Hubei province and explore the factors underlying these differences. Clinical characteristics and outcomes of hospitalised patients with COVID-19 treated in Hubei epicenter and outside Hubei non-epicenter: A Nationwide Analysis of China.
