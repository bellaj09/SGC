With the move to competency-based models of surgical training a number of assessment methods have been developed. Of these global rating scales have emerged as popular tools and several are specific to the assessment of arthroscopic skills. Our aim was to determine which one of a group of commonly used global rating scales demonstrated superiority in the assessment of simulated arthroscopic skills. Which Global Rating Scale? A Comparison of the ASSET BAKSSS and IGARS for the Assessment of Simulated Arthroscopic Skills.
