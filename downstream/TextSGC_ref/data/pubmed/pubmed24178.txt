Virtual reality surgical simulators VRSS have been showing themselves as a valuable tool in laparoscopy training and education. Taking in consideration the effectiveness of the VRSS new uses for this tool have been purposed. In sports warming up before exercise clearly shows benefit in performance. It is hypothesized that warming up in the VRSS before going to the operating room may show benefit in surgical performance. Does Warm-Up Training in a Virtual Reality Simulator Improve Surgical Performance? A Prospective Randomized Analysis.
