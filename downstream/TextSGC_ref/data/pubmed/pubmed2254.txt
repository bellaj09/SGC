We believe a point-of-care PoC device for the rapid detection of the 2019 novel Coronavirus SARS-CoV-2 is crucial and urgently needed. With this perspective we give suggestions regarding a potential candidate for the rapid detection of the coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 as well as factors for the preparedness and response to the outbreak of the COVID-19. 2019 Novel Coronavirus Disease COVID-19: Paving the Road for Rapid Detection and Point-of-Care Diagnostics.
