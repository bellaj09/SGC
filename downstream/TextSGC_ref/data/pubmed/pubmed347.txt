Ever since the first positive test was identified on January 21 2020 Washington State has been on the frontlines of the COVID-19 pandemic. Based on information obtained from Italian surgeons in Milan and given concerns regarding rising case numbers in Washington state we implemented new vascular surgery guidelines which canceled all non-emergent surgical procedures and involved significant changes to our inpatient and outpatient workflow. The consequences of these decisions are not yet understood. Emerging Practice Patterns in Vascular Surgery During the COVID-19 Pandemic.
