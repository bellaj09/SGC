Individuals living with Multiple Sclerosis MS often face progressive loss of function uncertainty and disruption to self-image and valued roles. Previous studies show that creative self-expression is valued by some people living with long-term illness yet its meaning for people living with MS is unclear. This research study explored the meanings of leisure-based visual art-making for people living with MS. "It gave me something big in my life to wonder and think about which took over the space\u2009\u2009and not MS": managing well-being in multiple sclerosis through art-making.
