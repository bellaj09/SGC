Coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 is a life-threatening respiratory condition caused by severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 and was initially detected in China in December 2019. Currently in Germany over 140000 cases of COVID-19 are confirmed. Here we report a nosocomial outbreak of SARS-CoV-2 infections in the pediatric dialysis unit of the University Hospital of Mnster UHM. First reported nosocomial outbreak of severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 in a pediatric dialysis unit.
