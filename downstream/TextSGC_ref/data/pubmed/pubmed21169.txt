Exergames are a new form of rehabilitation that combine the characteristics of physical exercise and the benefits of non-immersive virtual reality VR. Effects of this novel therapy in women fibromyalgia are still unknown. The objective was to evaluate the effects of exergame-based intervention on mobility skills balance and fear of falling in women with fibromyalgia. Exergames for women with fibromyalgia: a randomised controlled trial to evaluate the effects on mobility skills balance and fear of falling.
