Surgical ablation of select brain areas has been frequently used to alleviate psychological dependence on opiate drugs in certain countries. However ablative brain surgery was stopped in China in 2004 due to the related ethical controversy and possible side effects. Deep brain stimulation DBS a less invasive reversible and adjustable process of neuromodulation was adopted to attenuate relapses in studies of drug addiction. DBS of nucleus accumbens on heroin seeking behaviors in self-administering rats.
