As reported by the World Health Organization a novel coronavirus 2019-nCoV was identified as the causative virus of Wuhan pneumonia of unknown etiology by Chinese authorities on 7 January 2020. The virus was named as severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 by International Committee on Taxonomy of Viruses on 11 February 2020. This study aimed to develop a mathematical model for calculating the transmissibility of the virus. A mathematical model for simulating the phase-based transmissibility of a novel coronavirus.
