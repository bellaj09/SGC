The limitations in performing functional activities in children and adolescents with cerebral palsy are important. The use of virtual reality systems is a new treatment approach that reinforces task-oriented motor learning. The purpose of this guide is to study the impact of the use of virtual reality systems in the improvement and acquisition of functional skills and to evaluate the scientific evidence to determine the strength of recommendation of such interventions. Use of virtual reality systems as proprioception method in cerebral palsy: clinical practice guideline.
