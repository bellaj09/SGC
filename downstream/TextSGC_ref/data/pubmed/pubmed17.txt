In response to the outbreak of COVID-19 we set up a team to carry out sampling in the community. This enabled individuals to remain in self-isolation in their own homes and to prevent healthcare settings and services from being overwhelmed by admissions for sampling of suspected cases. There is evidence that this is a cost effective safe and necessary service to complement COVID-19 testing in hospitals.
