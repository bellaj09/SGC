"This is the first work exploring the application of new technologies concretely virtual reality to facilitate emotional processing in the treatment of Complicated Grief. Our research team has designed a virtual reality environment EMMAs World to foster the expression and processing of emotions. In this study the authors present a description of EMMAs World the clinical protocol and a case report. The treatment program was applied in eight sessions. We present a brief description of the session agendas including the techniques used. We offer short-term from pre-test to post-test and long-term 2- 6- and 12-month follow-ups efficacy data. Our results offer preliminary support of the use of EMMAs World for the treatment of Complicated Grief." Treatment of complicated grief using virtual reality: a case report.
