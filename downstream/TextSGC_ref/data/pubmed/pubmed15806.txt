To develop an artificial intelligence AI-based model for identifying patients with lymph node LN metastasis based on digital evaluation of primary tumors and train the model using cystectomy specimens available from The Cancer Genome Atlas TCGA Project; patients from our institution were included for validation of the leave-out test cohort. Multiresolution Application of Artificial Intelligence in Digital Pathology for Prediction of Positive Lymph Nodes From Primary Tumors in Bladder Cancer.
