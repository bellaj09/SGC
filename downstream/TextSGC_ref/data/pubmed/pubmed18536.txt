The purpose of this study is to evaluate the effects of a virtual reality training with BTS Nirvana BTS-N system in the cognitive and behavioral recovery in patients with Parkinson disease PD. The BTS-N is a semi-immersive therapy system used for motor and cognitive rehabilitation of patients with neurological diseases by creating virtual scenarios with which the patient may interact. "What About the Role of Virtual Reality in Parkinson Diseases Cognitive Rehabilitation? Preliminary Findings From a Randomized Clinical Trial."
