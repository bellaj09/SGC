Pain management in children often is inadequate and the single most common painful procedure in children who are hospitalized is needle procedures. Virtual reality VR is a promising and engaging intervention that may help to decrease anxiety and pain in children undergoing painful procedures. Our aim for this study is to investigate patient satisfaction and pain reduction by using a three-dimensional VR interactive game as a distraction. Immersive Virtual Reality for Pediatric Procedural Pain: A Randomized Clinical Trial.
