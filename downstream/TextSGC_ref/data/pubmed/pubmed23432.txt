The purpose of the current pilot study was to evaluate the acceptability and preliminary impact of using immersive virtual reality environments IVREs paired with a brief emotion regulation and risk reduction intervention ER + IVRE relative to this same intervention content paired with role-plays ER + RP. Moving Beyond Role-Play: Evaluating the Use of Virtual Reality to Teach Emotion Regulation for the Prevention of Adolescent Risk Behavior Within a Randomized Pilot Trial.
