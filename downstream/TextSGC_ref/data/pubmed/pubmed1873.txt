We describe two elderly patients evaluated at emergency departments for anosmia/dysgeusia in the absence of any other respiratory symptoms prior to or upon admission. In the current epidemiological context clinical and biological work-up led to a diagnosis of COVID-19 infection. Unfortunately one of the patients died during hospitalization but the other recovered and was discharged. Anosmia and Dysgeusia in the Absence of Other Respiratory Diseases: Should COVID-19 Infection Be Considered?
