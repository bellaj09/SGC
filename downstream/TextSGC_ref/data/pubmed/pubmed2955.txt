Coronaviruses are a large family of single positive-stranded enveloped RNA viruses that can infect many animal species and humans. Human coronaviruses can be divided based on their pathogenicity. The types with high pathogenicity including SARS-CoV MERS-CoV and current novel SARS-CoV2.1 Cross-species transmission is the most likely model of the initial transmission from bat to human.
