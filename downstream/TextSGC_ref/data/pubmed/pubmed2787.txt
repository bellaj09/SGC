While the outbreak of coronavirus disease 2019 COVID-19 has resulted in more than 100\u202f000 infected individuals in China and worldwide there are few reports on the association of severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 with ocular abnormalities. Understanding ocular manifestations of patients with COVID-19 by ophthalmologists and others may facilitate the diagnosis and prevention of transmission of the disease. Characteristics of Ocular Findings of Patients With Coronavirus Disease 2019 COVID-19 in Hubei Province China.
