This self-directed learning module explores the clinical application of new and evolving research frontiers in the field of neuroimaging neuroprotective agents and cognition-enhancing medications as applied to patients with brain injury. The current and potential clinical applications of robotic technology virtual reality and telemedicine in the field of physical medicine and rehabilitation is explored.
