The performance of tracheotomy is a common procedural request by critical care departments to the surgical services of general surgery thoracic surgery and otolaryngology - head & neck surgery. A Canadian Society of Otolaryngology - Head & Neck Surgery CSO-HNS task force was convened with multi-specialty involvement from otolaryngology-head & neck surgery general surgery critical care and anesthesiology to develop a set of recommendations for the performance of tracheotomies during the COVID-19 pandemic.
