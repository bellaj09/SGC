Advanced features are being added to telemedicine paradigms to enhance usability and usefulness. Virtual Interactive Presence VIP is a technology that allows a surgeon and patient to interact in a "merged reality" space to facilitate both verbal visual and manual interaction. In this clinical study a mobile VIP iOS application was introduced into routine post-operative orthopedic and neurosurgical care.
