The ongoing COVID-19 pandemic has infected nearly 400000 individuals with 17000 deaths since it was first identified in human populations in December 2019 in Wuhan China. No antiviral therapies or vaccines are available for their treatment or prevention. Passive immunization PI through broadly neutralizing antibodies that bind to the specific antigens of SARS-CoV 2 might be a potential solution to address the immediate health threat of COVID-19 pandemic while vaccines are being developed. The PI approach in treating COVID-19 is discussed herein including a summary of its historical applications to confront epidemics.
