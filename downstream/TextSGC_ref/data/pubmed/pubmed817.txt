Coronavirus disease of 2019 COVID-19 is a cause of significant morbidity and mortality worldwide. While cardiac injury has been demonstrated in critically ill COVID-19 patients the mechanism of injury remains unclear. Here we review our current knowledge of the biology of SARS-CoV-2 and the potential mechanisms of myocardial injury due to viral toxicities and host immune responses. Cardiovascular Complications in Patients with COVID-19: Consequences of Viral Toxicities and Host Immune Response.
