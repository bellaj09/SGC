The demonstration of competency in endoscopy is required prior to obtaining American Board of Surgery Certification. To demonstrate competency the resident must pass a national high-stakes cognitive test and a technical skills exam on a virtual reality simulator. The purpose of this preliminary study was to design a proficiency-based endoscopy simulation curriculum to meet this competency requirement. Development of a train-to-proficiency curriculum for the technical skills component of the fundamentals of endoscopic surgery exam.
