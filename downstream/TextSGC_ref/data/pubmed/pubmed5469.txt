The question of whether there is a continuum or a dichotomy among patients with schizophrenia SZ and bipolar disorders BD has not been clearly resolved and remains a challenge. Thus the identification of specific biomarkers of these disorders might be helpful. The present study investigated the volume of the corpus callosum CC and functional lateralization for language as potential biomarkers and their relationships in SZ and BD. Relationships between corpus callosum and language lateralization in patients with schizophrenia and bipolar disorders.
