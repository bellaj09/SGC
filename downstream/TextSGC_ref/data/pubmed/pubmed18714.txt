The occurrence of major emergencies often leads to environmental damage property damage health challenges and life threats. Despite the tremendous progress we have made in responding to the many challenges posed by disasters in recent years there are still many shortcomings. As an emerging technology widely used in recent years virtual reality VR technology is very suitable for many fields of disaster medicine such as basic education professional training psychotherapy etc. The purpose of this review article is to introduce the application of VR technology in the disaster medical field and prospect its trend in the future. Application of Virtual Reality Technology in Disaster Medicine.
