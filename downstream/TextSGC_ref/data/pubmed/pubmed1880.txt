The purpose of the current systematic review is to evaluate the efficacy of antiviral therapies in treatment of COVID-19. In addition clinical trials on the efficacy of antiviral therapies in the management of Severe Acute Respiratory Syndrome coronavirus SARS-Cov or Middle East Respiratory Syndrome coronavirus MERS-CoV have also been reviewed in order to identify potential treatment options for COVID-19. Antiviral therapy in management of COVID-19: a systematic review on current evidence.
