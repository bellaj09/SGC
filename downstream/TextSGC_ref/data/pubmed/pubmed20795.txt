"The first half of this paper documents the role of nature in healthcare environments and its impact on wellness with a particular focus on gardens. The second half presents a scientific evaluation of the role of gardens as a therapeutic intervention to optimise the clinical outcomes in patients with Alzheimers disease AD and dementia including a review of the innovative application of technologies alongside nature to promote cognitive rehabilitation in this particular patient population." "Therapeutic gardens as a design approach for optimising the healing environment of patients with Alzheimers disease and other dementias: A narrative review."
