To develop and validate a deep learning DL algorithm that predicts referable glaucomatous optic neuropathy GON and optic nerve head ONH features from color fundus images to determine the relative importance of these features in referral decisions by glaucoma specialists GSs and the algorithm and to compare the performance of the algorithm with eye care providers. Deep Learning and Glaucoma Specialists: The Relative Importance of Optic Disc Features to Predict Glaucoma Referral in Fundus Photographs.
