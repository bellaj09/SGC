The United States now has the highest death toll due to COVID-19. Many otolaryngology procedures including laryngoscopy bronchoscopy and esophagoscopy place otolaryngologists at increased risk of coronavirus transmission due to close contact with respiratory droplets and aerosolization from the procedure. The aim of this study is to provide an overview of guidelines on how to perform these procedures during the coronavirus pandemic.
