During the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic almost all European countries are adopting mass quarantine or isolation measures as a public health strategy to limit the spread of the contagion. Italy as the first European country to be deeply affected by such pandemic is currently approaching the fourth week of total lockdown with restrictive measures on the population and profound challenge for the national health-care system. Mass quarantine measures in the time of COVID-19 pandemic: psycho-social implications for chronic skin conditions and a call for qualitative studies.
