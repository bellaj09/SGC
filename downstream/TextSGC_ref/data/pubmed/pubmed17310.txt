"Organizational climate relates to how employees perceive and describe the characteristics of their employing organization. It has been found to have an impact on healthcare professionals and patients experiences of healthcare e.g. job satisfaction patient satisfaction as well as organizational outcomes e.g. employee productivity. This research used organizational theory to explore dynamics between health care professionals pharmacists doctors and nurses in mental health outpatients services for patients taking clozapine and the perceived influence on patient care." "The influence of organisational climate on care of patients with schizophrenia: a qualitative analysis of health care professionals views."
