The presence of metastatic lymph nodes is a prognostic indicator for patients with thyroid carcinomas and is an important determinant of clinical decision making. However evaluating neck lymph nodes requires experience and is labor- and time-intensive. Therefore the development of a computer-aided diagnosis CAD system to identify and differentiate metastatic lymph nodes may be useful. Deep Learning-Based Computer-Aided Diagnosis System for Localization and Diagnosis of Metastatic Lymph Nodes on Ultrasound: A Pilot Study.
