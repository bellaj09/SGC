Fear of flying FOF can be a serious problem for individuals who develop this condition and for military and civilian organizations that operate aircraft. The aim of this study was to compare the effectiveness of three treatments: bibliotherapy BIB without therapist contact; individualized virtual reality exposure therapy VRE; and cognitive behavior therapy CB. In addition we evaluated the effect of following up VRE and CB with 2 d of group cognitive-behavioral training GrCB. Fear of flying treatment methods: virtual reality exposure vs. cognitive behavioral therapy.
