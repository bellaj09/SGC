Virtual interactive presence VIP is a new technology that allows an individual to deliver real-time virtual assistance to another geographically remote individual via a standard Internet connection. The objectives of this pilot study were to evaluate the efficiency and performance of a VIP system implemented in an operating room setting determine the potential utility of the system for guidance of surgical procedures and assess the safety of the system.
