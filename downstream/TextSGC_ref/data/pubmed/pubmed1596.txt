On 11 March 2020 World Health Organization WHO declared the COVID-19 infection a pandemic. The risk of ischemic stroke may be higher in patients with COVID-19 infection similar to those with other respiratory tract infections. We present a comprehensive set of practice implications in a single document for clinicians caring for adult patients with acute ischemic stroke with confirmed or suspected COVID-19 infection. Management of acute ischemic stroke in patients with COVID-19 infection: Report of an international panel.
