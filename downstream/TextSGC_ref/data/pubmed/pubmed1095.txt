We report a familial cluster of 2019 novel coronavirus disease COVID-19 to evidence that a potential transmission of the COVID-19 during the incubation period. The first patient in this familial cluster was identified in presymptomatic period as a close contact of a confirmed patient. Five family members had close contact with the first patient during his incubation period four of them were confirmed to the severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2 SARS-CoV-2 infection in the subsequent sampling test. Transmission of COVID-19 in the terminal stage of incubation period: a familial cluster.
