"Studies in K-12 and college students show that their learning preferences have been strongly shaped by new media technologies like video games virtual reality environments the Internet and social networks. However there is no known research on medical students game experiences or attitudes towards new media technologies in medical education. This investigation seeks to elucidate medical student experiences and attitudes to see whether they warrant the development of new media teaching methods in medicine." Medical student attitudes toward video games and related new media technologies in medical education.
