Facial feminization surgery entails a series of surgical procedures that help the transwoman pass as their affirmed gender. Although virtual surgical planning with intraoperative cutting guides and custom plates have been shown to be helpful for craniomaxillofacial reconstruction they have not yet been studied for facial feminization surgery. The authors used cadaveric analysis for morphologic typing and to demonstrate the utility of virtual surgical planning in facial feminization surgery procedures. Osseous Transformation with Facial Feminization Surgery: Improved Anatomical Accuracy with Virtual Planning.
