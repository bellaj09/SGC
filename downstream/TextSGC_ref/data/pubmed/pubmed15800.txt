Deep endometriosis infiltrating the rectum may be managed by full thickness disc excision with the goal of preserving rectal function and avoiding low anterior rectal resection syndrome. Transanal staplers may be successfully used to remove rectal wall disc and concomitantly to perform rectal suture. The goal of the video article is to identify 10 steps which may render the procedure standardized and reproducible.
