The newly identified 2019-nCoV which appears to have originated in Wuhan the capital city of Hubei province in central China is spreading rapidly nationwide. A number of cases of neonates born to mothers with 2019-nCoV pneumonia have been recorded. However the clinical features of these cases have not been reported and there is no sufficient evidence for the proper prevention and control of 2019-nCoV infections in neonates. Clinical analysis of 10 neonates born to mothers with 2019-nCoV pneumonia.
