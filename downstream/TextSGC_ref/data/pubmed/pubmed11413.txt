The introduction of digital pathology into clinical practice has led to the development of clinical workflows with digital images in connection with pathology reports. Still most of the current work is time-consuming manual analysis of image areas at different scales. Links with data in the biomedical literature are rare and a need for search based on visual similarity within whole slide images WSIs exists. Deep Learning-Based Retrieval System for Gigapixel Histopathology Cases and the Open Access Literature.
