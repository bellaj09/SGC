"Surgical critical care SCC fellows are expected to receive training in critical care ultrasound CCUS but training is sporadic and there is no standardized curriculum to guide educators. Previous studies show wide variation in CCUS training during fellowship across specialties but SCC has been underrepresented. This study was performed to assess SCC program directors views regarding CCUS during fellowship training." Ultrasound Training in Surgical Critical Care Fellowship: A Survey of Program Directors.
