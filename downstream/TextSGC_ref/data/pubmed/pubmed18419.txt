A new patient monitoring technology called Visual Patient which transforms numerical and waveform data into a virtual model an avatar of the monitored patient has been shown to improve the perception of vital signs compared to conventional patient monitoring. In order to gain a deeper understanding of the opinions of potential future users regarding the new technology we have analyzed the answers of two large groups of anesthetists using two different study methods. User perceptions of avatar-based patient monitoring: a mixed qualitative and quantitative study.
