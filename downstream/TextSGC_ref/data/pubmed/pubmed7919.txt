Falls of individuals with dementia are frequent dangerous and costly. Early detection and access to the history of a fall is crucial for efficient care and secondary prevention in cognitively impaired individuals. However most falls remain unwitnessed events. Furthermore understanding why and how a fall occurred is a challenge. Video capture and secure transmission of real-world falls thus stands as a promising assistive tool. Reduction in Fall Rate in Dementia Managed Care Through Video Incident Review: Pilot Study.
