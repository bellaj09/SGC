A sudden cardiac arrest SCA occurs when the heart abruptly stops beating; because of the nature of SCA capturing data in the out-of-hospital setting from actual bystander response is difficult. Current technologies such as virtual reality VR allow the creation of scenarios programmed for heightened realism. No studies have used an immersive VR system to observe lay bystander response. Using an Immersive Virtual Reality System to Assess Lay Provider Response to an Unannounced Simulated Sudden Cardiac Arrest in the Out-of-Hospital Setting.
