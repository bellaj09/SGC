organization of a radiotherapy service during the covid-19 epidemic: experience of regional center of oncology of agadir, morocco. ¬© 2020 The College of RadiographersThis study proposes organization of the activity of a radiotherapy service during the pandemic COVID-19 period. Reliable circuits for staff as well as for patients are installed and treatment protocols are adapted to the current COVID-19 situation. Several scenarios are proposed to deal with any subsequent pandemic situation.
