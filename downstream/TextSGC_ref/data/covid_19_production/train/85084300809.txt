short-term effects of mitigation measures for the containment of the covid-19 outbreak: an experience from northern italy. Copyright ¬© 2020 Society for Disaster Medicine and Public Health, Inc.We evaluated the short-term effects of mitigation measures imposed by the Italian government on the first 10 municipalities affected by Sars-Cov-2 spread. Our results suggest that the effects of containment measures can be appreciated in about two weeks.
