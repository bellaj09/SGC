management of covid-19: a practical guideline for maternal and newborn health care providers in sub-saharan africa. ¬© 2020, ¬© 2020 Informa UK Limited, trading as Taylor & Francis Group.COVID-19 is a pandemic that is currently ravaging the world. Infection rate is steadily increasing in Sub-Saharan Africa. Pregnant women and their infants may suffer severe illnesses due to their lower immunity. This guideline prepares and equips clinicians working in the maternal and newborn sections in the sub-region to manage COVID-19 during pregnancy and childbirth.
