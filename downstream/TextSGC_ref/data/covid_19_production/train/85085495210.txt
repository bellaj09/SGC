the proportion of covid-19 cases that are asymptomatic in south korea: comment on nishiura et al. ¬© 2020 The Author(s)The Korean Centers for Disease Control & Prevention reports the asymptomatic case proportion for COVID-19 is 33.3%. The asymptomatic case proportion in South Korea closely approximates the rate of 30.8% reported by Nishiura et al. (2020) among Japanese nationals returning from abroad.
