All rights reserved.The Covid-19 emergency has led to significant changes in the workings of the Department of Neuropsychology at the National Hospital for Neurology and Neurosurgery. Here, we describe how the Department has responded to the challenges posed by the emergency by rapidly redesigning its services in order to support staff, patients and their families and carers.
