All rights reserved.The coronavirus disease 2019 (COVID-19) has currently caused a global health threat. Ophthalmic care settings pose a risk of cross-infection. The preventive strategy regarding ophthalmic practice in response to the outbreak is discussed. This is the first report form a tertiary eye hospital in Iran and second of its kind worldwide.
