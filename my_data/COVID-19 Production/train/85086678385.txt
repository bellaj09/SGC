the psychosocial impact of covid-19 pandemic in italy: a lesson for mental health prevention in the first severely hit european country. ¬© 2020 American Psychological Association.Italy was the first European country severely hit by the COVID-19 pandemic. While the containment measures were relatively effective in the acute phase, the current postemergency phase addressing the long-term psychosocial consequences is the key challenge for our healthcare system, where the importance of mental health prevention is not sufficiently recognized.
