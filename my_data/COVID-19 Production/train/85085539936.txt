New York.The respiratory disease caused by the novel coronavirus (SARS-CoV-2) presents a major challenge for health care systems. Based on the numerous radiology studies that have already been published and our own initial experience, patients can be divided into 3 groups based on CT morphology during the pandemic: 1. CT finding suggestive of COVID-19 pneumonia, 2. CT finding consistent with viral pneumonia, COVID-19 pneumonia possible, and 3. COVID-19 pneumonia not likely based on CT findings.
