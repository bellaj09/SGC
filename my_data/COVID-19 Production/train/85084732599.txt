All rights reserved.Epidemics and Pandemics have impacted global economies and had always been a real threat to the entire world. The Indian stock market had reacted during each outbreak. The recent pandemic i.e Covid-19 has affected the economies very badly due to the uncertainty prevailing on its future. Till date the vaccine has not been developed by various developed countries which claim to have a good medical facility and infrastructure. However, the world has witnessed several such outbreaks. One common thing witnessed in such crisis is the knee-jerk reaction of the financial markets and forcing markets to touch its new bottoms. The paper is an attempt to study the impact of outbreaks on BSE/Sensex and reactions of various BSE indices during and post outbreaks.
